Design Philosophy

The Flower of Life is a pattern of equal circles overlapping. No circle is more important than another. Every intersection creates something new. This is exactly how civic infrastructure works — organizations, people, policies, and places overlapping to create community.

As engagement deepens, more circles appear. A single circle is reading an article. Two overlapping is making a connection. The full flower is sustained organizing. The icon literally grows with you.

Database images are used for all thumbnails where available. Where no image exists, the fallback is the relevant pathway color as a subtle gradient header — never a generic placeholder, never an emoji.
