Our Health

Community Health Workers
Are Changing Everything

They're not doctors. They're your neighbors. And they're bridging a gap the system never could.

Community health workers are trusted members of their communities who serve as vital links between healthcare systems and the people they serve. In Houston, CHW programs are demonstrating real, measurable impact -- particularly in neighborhoods that have been historically underserved.

These programs focus on culturally competent outreach, health literacy, care coordination, and connection to social services. The evidence is clear: communities with active CHW programs see better chronic disease outcomes, higher vaccination rates, and fewer emergency room visits.

Source: Houston Chronicle, Feb 2026
