Finding a therapist in Houston: A step-by-step guide

Insurance, sliding scale, telehealth — all your options explained simply.

Mental Health Guide 5 min read

Finding a therapist can feel overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Houston has more mental health resources than most cities — the challenge is knowing where to start.

Step 1: Know your options

There are three main paths to finding a therapist in Houston: through your insurance, through community health centers with sliding scale fees, or through telehealth platforms.

Step 2: Check your insurance

If you have insurance, your plan's website will have a provider directory. Texas law requires mental health parity — your copay for therapy should be the same as for a regular doctor visit.

Step 3: Sliding scale options

No insurance? Legacy Community Health, Avenue 360, and The Harris Center all offer therapy on a sliding scale based on income. Typical range: $5-$40 per session.

Want to find a provider now? You can search by location, insurance, or specialty.
