How Houston's Community Health Workers Are Transforming Neighborhood Care

The Growing Role of Community Health Workers

Community health workers (CHWs) are trusted members of the communities they serve. They help navigate the healthcare system, connect residents with preventive services, and provide culturally sensitive health education.
