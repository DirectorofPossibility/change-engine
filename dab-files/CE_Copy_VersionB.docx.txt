HOOK

HEADLINE


	The Civic Knowledge Galaxy.
	

SUBHEAD


	Not a list. Not a directory. A living map of how it all fits together.
	

INTRO

Those connections are real. Until now, nobody was showing them to you.

**Opportunities** — Ways to show up and get involved.

THE POINT


	Most platforms show you one thing. We show you how everything connects. That's the difference between a list and a map.

HIGH
	/manual
User Manual
	

HOOK


	You don't need a guide to use this. But here's one anyway.
	

HEADLINE


	How Community Exchange works.
	

SUBHEAD


	Start anywhere. The platform will show you what connects.
	

START WITH YOUR ADDRESS


	The fastest way in is your address. Type it into Civic Compass and we'll show you every elected official who represents you — city council, county commissioner, state rep, state senator, U.S. representative, U.S. senator.


All of them. One place. Right now.
	

BROWSE BY WHAT MATTERS TO YOU


	Not sure where to start? Pick a pathway. Seven themes — housing, health, education, environment, economy, safety, civic life. Pick the one that's closest to what you care about.


Inside each pathway: organizations working on it, services available for it, news about it, officials responsible for it.
	

SEARCH FOR ANYTHING


	Use the search bar. A name, a place, an issue, an organization. Use the tabs to filter by type. Everything updates daily.
	

FOLLOW WHAT'S HAPPENING


	The live dashboard shows what's moving right now — legislation, community activity, events you can attend.


Three Good Things shows you three positive civic stories every day.
	

SAVE WHAT MATTERS


	Create a free account to save officials, organizations, and articles. Set your language. Get notified about things in your area.


You don't need an account. But it helps if you want to keep track.
	

LANGUAGES


	English, Spanish, and Vietnamese. Switch in the top navigation or in your settings.


All service content is reviewed by a human editor before it publishes in any language.
	

WAYFINDER


	At the bottom of every page — every official, every organization, every service — you'll see Wayfinder. It shows you what's connected to what you're looking at. Three hops out from any starting point.


It's how you go from 'I need help with rent' to 'here are the organizations, the officials responsible, and the policy that funds it' — without having to already know where to look.
	

CLOSING


	That's it. Start with what you know. The platform shows you the rest.


Questions? Reach us at hello@changeengine.us.
